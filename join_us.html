Interested in join the club?

We take in new members at the beginning of each term (Fall, Winter)

Membership sales are offered by Heart House, and are conducted before the semester starts, but if you missed the sale window, please sign up for our waiting list.

Please provide your student number and email address. We will get back to you as soon as we have more openings.

To help maintain fairness, waitlist entries are added in 24-hour periods. Students added at 12am will have no advantage over students added any other time in the same day. Entries on each day are sorted randomly.
