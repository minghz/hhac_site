Learn archery

Archery is an amazing activity to take up at any age. It will help you discover yourself and what you are truly capable of.

We offer quality coaching at an indoor shooting range at the heart of downtown Toronto. We teach our members the basics of shooting as well as help develop their skills in a friendly environment. We also support members enter more competitive streams.

Got questions? We got you covered. Check out our FAQ.

Activities and tournaments

In addition to our normal range hours, we offer a variety of activities throughout the year, including:

  • Targeting baloons, plushies, cards, zombies, etc
  • Haloween Fun Shoot
  • The Winter Tournament
  • The Valentines Day Fun Shoot
  • Club Championship

Also, every February the club hosts the annual Inter-University Archery Tournament (IUAT). Archers from UofT, York, Queens, and Waterloo compete against each other in the various categories.

Hang out with us

We accept new members at the start of each term (fall and winter). When your application is approved, you will also need to purchase a club membership from Heart House.

The club is open every Tuesday and Thursday from 5pm to 8pm.

Location

The range is located at the Multidisciplinary Activity room within Heart House, in the heart of Downtown Toronto
