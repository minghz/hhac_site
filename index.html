Archery Club

@ Heart House

Learn to shoot!

Learning archery is an amazing activity to take up at any age and helps you to discover yourself and what you are truly capable of.

We offer quality coaching at an indoor shooting range at the heart of downtown Toronto. We teach our members the basics of shooting as well as help develop their skills in a friendly environment. We also support members enter more competitive streams.

Got questions? We got you covered. Check out our FAQ.

Fun activities and tournaments!

We host fun-shoots over the year, targeting baloons, plushies, cards, zombies, etc. We host the Haloween Fun Shoot, The Winter Tournament, The Valentines Day Fun Shoot, and the Club Championship.

Every February, HHAC hosts the annual Inter-University Archery Tournament (IUAT)! Archers from UofT, York, Queens, and Waterloo compete against each other in the various categories.

Hang out with us!

To join the club, checkout this page for more instructions. We accept new members at the start of each term (fall and winter). When your application is approved, you will also need to purchase a club membership from Heart House.

The club is open every Tuesday and Thursday from 5pm to 8pm. For more information, check out our schedule.

For members, you can just drop by at any time for however long you would like!
