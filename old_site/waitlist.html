Info

The waitlist is for students who missed the initial membership sales for either semester. To add yourself to the waitlist, you'll need to provide your student number and email address so we can contact you when we have openings.

To help maintain fairness, waitlist entries are added in 24-hour periods. Students added at 12am will have no advantage over students added any other time in the same day. Entries on each day are sorted randomly.

If you need help modifying any information in the waitlist information, please email us.
