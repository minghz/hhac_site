Getting coached

HHAC is fortunate to have many qualified coaches at the facility on a regular basis. Many of them have different backgrounds and experiences upon which to draw knowledge, and all of them have gone through training programs including the National Coaching Certification Program (NCCP). Approach one from time to time to get some feedback on your performance. Don't be shy, they usually don't bite.

Becoming a coach

If you're interested in becoming a coach, check out Archery Canada or ask a Range Officer about the in-house NCCP clinic offered every spring.
