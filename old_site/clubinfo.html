New Members

The Hart House Archery Club is open to adults of any age holding a Hart House membership. University of Toronto students are automatically members of Hart House, but everyone else will need to purchase a Clubs Membership or Fitness Center Membership (Hart House Memberships).

New memberships will be made available twice a year, once near the start of the Fall semester and then again near the start of the Winter semester. Memberships are sold at the Hub (front desk) of Hart House. Please check our announcements for specific dates and times

All new members are required to sign up for a 30 minute introductory lesson. Multiple lesson slots are available when you purchase your membership. Even experienced archers must attend an introductory lesson, at least to learn the specific rules of our range.

Dates and Hours

The club is open every Tuesday and Thursday from 5pm to 8pm. Hart House Archery Club members are welcome to shoot for as long as they wish during these hours. Attendance is not tracked, so members need not participate for all six hours every week.

The club only operates during official University of Toronto class dates for the Spring and Winter semesters. The club may not be open during holidays, reading periods, or other dates when classes are not officially scheduled.

Equipment

The club offers equipment for archers of all experience levels and shooting styles. Beginners are equipped with our hand-crafted, custom-made King's Pawn bows. These bows are extremely light-weight and forgiving for novice shooters. We also offer traditional longbows, olympic recurves, and modern compounds for more advanced shooters.

We also offer all the necessary gear to get shooting. There are fully-fletched arrows, including extra-long arrows for those with a longer-than-average draw length. There are also finger tabs and arm guards to protect your arms and fingers while you shoot.

If you're not sure what equipment you need, or just looking for suggestions, our coaches are more than happy to help! Feel free to ask us anything at the range if you're unsure.

Archery classes

Archery classes are offered separately by Hart House, and are not affiliated with the club. Do note that we offer preferential waitlist entry to those who have completed archery classes offered by Hart House.

LEVEL I Monday Night Classes Excellent for beginners wanting to learn the basic skills. During this 5-week session, the archer will receive an initial safety lesson, and one-on-one coaching in 50minute classes.

LEVEL II Wednesday Night Classes A continuation of the Monday night classes, for archers wanting to hone their skills further. The Level II classes will be shooting at greater distances and include an exercise component during the 80minute class. Please note that the Level II Wednesday Class is for returning members only.
